About the Podcast

Stories from the drug development world — from discovery to market.

VITA1001 Modern Pharma is a podcast featuring modern pharma career journeys. We sit down with scientists, clinicians, CMC and QA/QC professionals, project managers, business development specialists and more to explore how medicines move from idea to patient — and the people behind every step.

Your Hosts

Sue

Co-host of VITA1001 Modern Pharma. Passionate about sharing the real stories behind drug development and connecting professionals across the pharma industry.

Iris

Co-host of VITA1001 Modern Pharma. Dedicated to exploring career paths in the pharmaceutical world and bringing insightful conversations to our listeners.

What We Cover

Drug Discovery & R&D

Early-stage research, target selection, formulation development, and the journey from bench to IND.

Clinical Operations

Trial management, protocol design, site monitoring, and the people who make clinical trials happen.

CMC & Quality

Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls; QA/QC processes; GMP compliance; and metrology in pharma.

Business & Career

Business development, project management, career transitions, MBA value, and navigating the pharma industry.

AI & Innovation

AI in drug discovery, bioinformatics, biosensors, CGM technology, and the future of pharma innovation.

Traditional Medicine

Bridging traditional Chinese medicine with modern pharmaceutical science and innovation.
