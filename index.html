About the Podcast

VITA1001 Modern Pharma is hosted by Sue and Iris. We feature modern pharma career journeys — real stories from the drug development world, from discovery to market.

We sit down with scientists, clinicians, CMC and QA/QC professionals, project managers, BD specialists, and more to explore how medicines move from idea to patient — and the people behind every step.
