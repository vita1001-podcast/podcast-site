About the Podcast

VITA1001 Modern Pharma is a podcast featuring modern pharma career journeys. We sit down with the people who make drug development happen — scientists, clinicians, CMC and QA/QC professionals, project managers, and more.

Each episode explores how medicines move from idea to patient, and the real stories of the people behind every step of the journey.
